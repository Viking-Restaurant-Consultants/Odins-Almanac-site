Prep & Plate

Color-Coded Labeling System

A patent-pending, automated food safety labeling and monitoring system that elevates compliance and quality assurance. Transforms food safety from a reactive chore into a proactive, automated workflow—reducing compliance violations, improving staff clarity, and providing measurable ROI.

FDA-Compliant Labeling
Generates labels with discard times, allergens, and storage instructions for every prepared item automatically.
Color-Coded Expiration Alerts
Dynamic color coding (red/yellow/green) signals expiration proximity—red for expired, yellow for near-expiry, green for safe.
Shelf Life Buffer
Adds a 2–3 day safety margin to expiration dates, reducing spoilage and risk with conservative compliance logic.
Automated Alerts
Instantly notifies managers when quality standards are not met, integrating with email, SMS, and AI agent alerts.
Daily Line Check Integration
Connects labeling and expiration tracking directly to daily line checks for every shift compliance and accountability.
Audit Trails & Corrective Action
Logs violations, tracks corrective actions, and escalates unresolved issues for regulatory documentation and legal protection.
Tablet/iPad Access
Supports hardware for consulting clients who prefer non-phone usage with intuitive touch interfaces.

Odin's Eye

Financial & Inventory Automation

Patent-pending financial and inventory management platform that turns operational chaos into clarity— the "icing on the cake" for restaurant profitability. Automates complex P&L reporting, optimizes inventory levels, and saves operators millions in labor costs through intelligent automation.

Integrated P&L Creator
Instantly builds locked, formula-driven profit & loss statements from raw trial balances and operational data with automated calculations.
Recipe Creator & Costing
Builds, stores, and scales recipes with ingredient-level costing and yield calculations, optimizing menu engineering.
Par Creator & Optimization
Automatically calculates ideal inventory levels based on historical sales, seasonality, and forecasted demand.
POS-Integrated Ordering
Automates vendor ordering by analyzing real-time sales and inventory data, generating recommended order quantities.
Low Par Alerts
Notifies managers when inventory drops below optimal levels, ensuring timely replenishment and preventing stockouts.
Multi-POS Synchronization
Seamlessly connects to any point-of-sale system for unified inventory, sales, and compliance tracking.
AI-Driven Analytics
Detects patterns, predicts risks, and optimizes stock levels to maximize profit margins with intelligent insights.
Role-Based Access Control
Seven-tier permission system for secure, multi-location management with granular access controls.
